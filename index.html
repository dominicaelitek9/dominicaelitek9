Our Policies

To ensure structural safety, consistency, and clean health settings for all handlers and dogs at our facility, please review the frameworks below:

  • Evaluation Mandate: All K9 prospects booking intermediate or protection tracks must undergo an initial drive profiling evaluation.
  • Vaccination Rules: All dogs visiting or boarding must possess up-to-date health books showing DHLPP and Rabies coverage.
  • Reservation Framework: Puppy deposits are structured to hold your selection sequence and are non-refundable.

Business Policies

To maintain our world-class standard of safety, structure, and operational excellence, all clients must strictly adhere to the following business frameworks:

Health and Vaccination Requirements

  • All canines entering our facility for daycare, boarding, or training must be fully up-to-date on required vaccinations (including Parvovirus, Distemper, and Rabies).
  • Proof of recent veterinary health assessment must be submitted prior to the evaluation check-in.
  • Dogs showing clear signs of contagious illness, parasites, or open, untreated injuries will not be accepted into care.

Booking, Deposits, and Cancellations

  • A non-refundable deposit is required to secure time slots for advanced boarding and multi-week training programs.
  • Cancellations or training schedule modifications must be submitted at least 48 hours in advance to avoid session forfeiture fees.

Safety and Assessment

  • All dogs undergo an initial temperament assessment to safely classify their social dynamics and drive parameters.
  • Dominica Elite K9 Services reserves the right to decline or alter service profiles for dogs deemed unsafe or unsuited for collective environment programs without structured tactical handlers.

Payments & Secure Checkout

Information on processing invoices, digital deposit methods, and retaining service slot allocations can be managed directly through our team.
